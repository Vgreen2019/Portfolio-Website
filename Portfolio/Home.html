Cooking...

I love being able to express my creativity with things. One opportunity for this is with cooking. From a young age I have always loved cooking things and that stayed with me into adulthood. I love the idea of people coming together to share a delicious meal. My favorite part about cooking is the combination of all the different spices and different flavors that can be added to create the best flavor profiles.

In my opinion a good cook can take any boring meal, big or small, dress it up and make it deliciousSometimes I like to try foods and figure what all was added to it by just tasting it. I also love to challenge myself by picking onr or two ingredients and designing a whole meal around it. Sometimes I surpirse myself with how good things turn out!

Traveling...

With most of my extended family being located in the south, I was introduced to traveling at an early age. When I left for college, I even made it my annual ritual to visit atleast one new place each year. After graduation, there really was nothing holding me back. I traveled out of the country to Madrid, Lisbon, Porto in Portugal and Spain and it was such an amazing experience. Being able to immerse yourself into a culture and meet others from different backgrounds was the highlight of my experience.

Hometown...

I often refer to Detroit, MI as my hometown but I actually am from a smaller city located inside of Detroit called Highland Park. Detroit and Highland Park started off as two separate cities but Detroit continued to grow until it surrounded Highland Park. Highland Park began as a small farming community back in 1818. In 1907, Henry Ford bought 160 acres and completed construction of the first automobile plant in that area. It was the second American production facility for the Model T automobile and the first factory in history to assemble cars on a moving assembly line. Although, Highland Park has become alot less know over the years the city still has a sense of community.
